Delete Your WeCare Account

Last updated: January 2025

If you wish to delete your WeCare account, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Account Deletion

You can request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data by sending an email to:

support@wecareapp.com

Your email must include:

  • Registered Email ID
  • Role (Patient / Doctor)
  • Account deletion request

What Happens After Deletion?

  • Your account will be removed from the system
  • Your login credentials will be erased
  • Your chat history and any temporary MRI logs will be deleted

The deletion process may take up to 48 hours.

Need Help?

For assistance, contact:

support@wecareapp.com
