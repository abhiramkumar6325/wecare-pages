Privacy Policy for WeCare: Tumor Prediction

Last updated: January 2025

WeCare: Tumor Prediction (“WeCare”, “the App”, “we”, “our”) is an AI-assisted medical support application that helps users generate preliminary MRI tumor predictions. Your privacy and data security are extremely important to us.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

WeCare does NOT collect, store, or share:

  • MRI images
  • Medical reports
  • Personal identifiers (name, phone, address)
  • Location information

All MRI processing happens temporarily during prediction. No data is stored on our servers.

2. Account Information

Users may create accounts as Patients or Doctors. The following may be stored securely:

  • Email
  • Password (encrypted)

3. How We Use Data

  • To authenticate users
  • To generate MRI prediction reports
  • To enable chat communication between patients and doctors

4. Federated Learning

The app may use Federated Learning to improve AI model accuracy. No identifiable data or MRI images are uploaded; only anonymous model updates are shared.

5. Third-Party Sharing

WeCare does not share user data with any third parties.

6. Data Deletion

Users may request permanent deletion of their account and associated data at any time via:

  • Delete Account page
  • Email request

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:

support@wecareapp.com
